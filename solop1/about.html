Jake's Top 3 Photos

  1. Rushing Water
    I really like this photo, just the way the light reflects off the water to create this nice effect that I can only really equate to a sunset is extremely satisfying. This has been my favorite photo for a while now and I don't know if I'll ever top it.
  2. Resturaunt Corner
    This was one of my first projects from my photography class, I think it looks pretty good, I am quite happy with how the lighting and angle ended up looking.
  3. Dew In The Morning
    Despite the name, I think I took this photo at around noon. It's a pretty solid looking photo, and I'm pleased with the end result. Though I feel like it's a bit too obvious I upped the saturation on this one.
